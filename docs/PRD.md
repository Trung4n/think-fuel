# ThinkFuel - Product Requirement Document

## 1. Product Overview

ThinkFuel is an AI-powered learning platform designed to help students learn effectively **without becoming dependent on AI**.

Instead of directly giving answers, the system encourages active thinking, problem solving, and independent learning through a reward-and-limitation mechanism called **Brain Fuel**.

The more responsibly a student uses AI, the better assistance they receive.
The more they rely on AI for shortcuts, the more limited AI support becomes.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Current AI tools create several problems in education:

- Students ask for answers instead of learning the process
- Overdependence reduces critical thinking ability
- Teachers cannot measure real understanding
- AI gives equal support to lazy and active learners
- Students gradually lose confidence solving problems alone

Schools need a smarter system where AI develops thinking instead of replacing it.

---

## 3. Product Vision

## 6. Core Product Concept

Each student owns a resource called **Brain Fuel**.

Brain Fuel represents how much AI assistance the student can use.

AI usage consumes Brain Fuel.

Independent learning restores Brain Fuel.

Low Brain Fuel changes AI behavior from giving help to forcing reflection.
